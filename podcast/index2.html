Meet our Hosts

Shaun

The Skeptic

Shaun is a once and future English professor, author, aspiring voiceover artist, and unrepentant grammar fanatic currently at work on his first novel. He’s had short fiction and essays appear in multiple venues, including Dark Fire Fiction, Bartleby Snopes, and Bewildering Stories.

Phil

The Scientist

Phil is...complicated. He is a PhD in physics and a college professor; in addition, he has been a professional magician for nearly 20 years. It gets stranger, but don't worry, others agree: Phil was once classified by a creative writing professor as "an unrealistic character."

Dave

The Angry Guy

Dave DiOrio: Father of two, husband of one, claimant of zero on his W-4, enjoys all manner of sports, competition reality shows and is an expert pasta sauce (not gravy you savages) concocter. This Nintendo Fan boy has been referred to as “Angry Dave” on several hundred occasions. If you like irrational anger, you’re gonna identify with Dave.

Jill

The Eclectic

Our New Addition!
Jill is The Whiparound’s resident single person and proud owner of ovaries. She is a writer, producer and educator. While media - specifically television and advertising – is her main discipline, she is highly knowledgeable on all things random. Her fondness for naps cannot be overstated.

Episode 11: “It’s Time to End the Year OUR Way!”

2017, at long last, comes crashing down, and The Whiparound is here to stab it and pose heroically! Jill, Shaun, and Phil all brought their particular segments to bear on sending off a year that was TOTALLY…roughly 365 days long. Science, pop culture, and politics all fall under the dazzling wit of your hosts—

Jill runs down the many fixes, physical, emotional, and otherwise of 2017, Phil’s research plunges the gang into an impromptu PRISON DRAFT, and Shaun raises the idea of a super-fun Contest: put YOUR 2017 experience into a GIF and send it our way, he says!

AND BEST OF ALL: Dave calls into the show with a VERY special announcement you’ve just gotta hear! Should auld acquaintance be forgot, have no fear, just bring THE WHIPAROUND to mind!!
