About Me

After spending over 10 years working in theatre and costuming (3 of those years spent commuting daily to NYC), I decided it was time for a change of pace. Using resources available online, I taught myself HTML and CSS and a smidgeon of JavaScript, and learned to make websites. I started freelance creating sites on the side until I was hired full-time for these skills.

In my new role, I learned about digital and inbound marketing and redesigned the company website, becoming an expert in the CMS Joomla along the way. On transition to the programming team, I had the opportunity to learn Bootstrap, Sass, Angular, Django, and Python while contributing to several apps being built in-house. On top of this, I built that app's website.

What's next? I'd like to learn enough to become an effective full-stack developer, and to that end am working on Python and the trickier frameworks. In the meantime, I'm working on becoming excellent at Javascript and designing as many different kinds of sites as I can.
