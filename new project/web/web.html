Services We Provide

La Porta Web Services specializes in web design and construction -- see below for examples of completed sites. We don't stop at web sites though! La Porta Web Services also handles social media, from setup of your desired platform to regular updates of the pages, as well as podcast hosting -- setting up your account, posting episodes, and keeping things up to date on iTunes and GooglePlay -- all you need to do is record and provide show notes and imagery!

The Story

After spending over 10 years working in theatre and costuming, I decided it was time for a change of pace. Using resources available online, I taught myself HTML and CSS and a smidgeon of JavaScript, and learned to make websites. I started creating sites on a freelance basis; eventually I was hired full-time for these skills, but my business lives on.

In my new role, I learned about digital and inbound marketing and redesigned the company website, becoming an expert in the CMS Joomla, SEO, and marketing strategies along the way. On transition to the programming team, I had the opportunity to learn Bootstrap, Sass, Angular, Django, and Python while contributing to several apps being built in-house. On top of this, I built the website for the company's first app.

What's next? I'd like to learn enough to become an effective full-stack developer, and to that end am working on Python and the trickier frameworks. In the meantime, I'm working on becoming excellent at Javascript and designing as many different kinds of sites as I can.
