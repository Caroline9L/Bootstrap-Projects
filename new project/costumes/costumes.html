Caroline's Costumes

For almost 2 decades, I have devoted my time and energy to costumes. From my first days of steaming tutus for college dance wardrobe, I have worked on honing my craft, perfecting my skills and learning as many new ones as I can. My love of costumes has taken me all over, but always back to the workroom, where I've met the most interesting people and collaborated on the most exciting projects.

 

Costume work

In the two decades in which I have been costuming professionally, I have worked in a variety of settings on a variety of costumes. With Muhlenberg College, I ran an academic theatre and dance costume shop whose builds were never the same twice; with the Shakespeare Theatre of NJ we did more with less, reinterpreting the classics in new and exciting ways; at Parsons-Meares Ltd, we brought to life Broadway and other commercial projects at a vast scale, while never comprimising quality; with Papermill Playhouse, I led a team that breathed life into the shows headed for the Great White Way; in my own workshop, I create new characters and collaborate with others to make dreams come true.

I take on projects from individuals as well as schools and theatre companies; email me to collaborate or to learn more about my credentials.

The Story

After spending over 10 years working in theatre and costuming, including a prolonged period commuting daily from eastern PA into Manhattan and Queens (3 hours in one direction), I bid the full-time theatre world farewell in favor of an office job closer to home.

This does not, however, mean that costuming is in my past. From my home studio, I am still a comfortable commute into NYC while enjoying lower overhead costs outside of the metropolitan area, and more importantly, a renewed enthusiasm for creating imaginative, well-constructed garments.

I am available on a freelance basis to undertake builds large and small, at my place or yours. My experience includes work for Beyonce, The Lion King, Marc Jacobs, Disney on Ice, and magazine shoot styling, as well as custom creations for brides, cosplayers, and magicians. Contact me today to discuss your project.
