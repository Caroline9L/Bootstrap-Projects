Artist and creator, medium not important

I came to web design and development late, and am so sad that I've missed out on this passion for so much of my life. I love creating web sites and apps that reflect the personality of the owner and brand. Using code to manipulate what appears on screen is like magic to me, and yet another way I have discovered to build a finished product from scratch! Check out my web development page for examples of some of the projects I've worked on so far.

My first love and career is costuming. I have an MFA in costume design, and have worked for theatres in academia, regional production, and even on Broadway! Though I no longer work full-time in the industry, I adore being commissioned for side projects; the garments I am able to create in my free time allow me to stretch my knowledge and imagination to the limit. Visit my Costumes page for examples of the garments I've built, and feel free to get in touch if you need something made!
