About Me

Artist and creator, medium not important.

Web design and development is my new love and passion! I love creating web sites that reflect the personality of the owner and brand. Using code to manipulate what appears on screen is like magic to me, and yet another way I love to build from scratch!

My first love and career is costumeing. I have an MFA in costume design, and have worked for theatres in academia, regional production, and even on Broadway! I adore my side projects these days; the garments I am allowed to create allow me to stretch my knowledge and imagination to the limit.
