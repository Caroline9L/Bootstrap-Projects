Caroline's Costumes

For almost 2 decades, I have devoted my time and energy to costumes. From my first days of steaming tutus for college dance wardrobe, I have worked on honing my craft, perfecting my skills and learning as many new ones as I can. My love of costumes has taken me all over, but always back to the workroom, where I've met the most interesting people and collaborated on the most exciting projects.

 

Costume work

In the two decades in which I have been costuming professionally, I have worked in a variety of settings on a variety of costumes. With Muhlenberg College, I ran an academic costume shop whose builds were never the same twice; with the Shakespeare Theatre of NJ we did more with less, reinterpreting the classics in new and exciting ways; at Parsons-Meares, we brought to life Broadway and other commercial projects at a vast scale, while never comprimising quality; with Papermill Playhouse, I led a team that breathed life into the shows headed for the Great White Way; and in my own workshop, I create new characters and collaborate to make dreams come true.

I take on projects from individuals as well as schools and theatre companies; email me to collaborate or to learn more about my credentials.
